CAN’T REALLY GO WRONG WITH PIZZA

When the craving hits, and believe that it will, you will go to great lengths to be able to enjoy a Piazo Top just one more time.

Baes Quarts

The pizza Baes is an inch thick and as drawn as my unfinished term paper. The sauce tastes like it came out of a can, a cheap one, and the topping is generous but greasy.

Potamo Turn

The pizza is as big as the wheels on your truck. It's thin crust with feta cheese, potato lines and fresh tomatoes- a “Greek” as they call it.

Herbizza

It's pizzeria perfect. How could it be anything else from heaven. Even herbs with three cheese toppings in the dough for the sake of your glowing skin.

Pepperino

American variety of salami, made from cured pork and beef mixed together and seasoned with paprika.

Fajjitza

Creamy feta, salty olives, artichokes and roasted red pepper to satisfy your hunger and sent you the taste buds for a loop.

Margizza

Wholegrain dough in bread machine, bag of homemade vegetable tomato sauce out of the freezer, tin of black pitted olives to slice, grate mozzarella cheese, roll dough on greased, floured tray, add toppings.
